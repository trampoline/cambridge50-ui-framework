Cambridge 50

  • Rankings
  • Charts

Cambridge 50 was put together by Cambridge business and academic community to support the economic growth in Cambridge by making it easy and transparent to identify our fastest growing companies and who you know in them so it is easier for you to invest, work or partner with us.

Here you can find our most innovative companies, as defined by the number of customer orders they receive and the by number of people they employ.

All of them are growing at least 20% per annum and you can also sort this by life sciences, physical sciences, wireless and clean tech sectors if you are in those sectors. You can also, at a glance see how many companies already have reached revenues of £ 1Million, 10 M, 25M, 100M, 250M, 500M and a Billion.
